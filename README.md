# Team_Rakshak
RakshakLink is an AI-powered emergency response app that connects users, ambulances, and hospitals in real time. It enables quick SOS alerts, smart ambulance dispatch, live bed tracking, and AI triage to ensure faster treatment during critical “Golden Hour” situations.
RakshakLink – The Emergency & Health Response Bridge 🚑

RakshakLink is an AI-powered emergency response system designed to bridge the critical gap between citizens, ambulances, and hospitals during the “Golden Hour” of medical emergencies. In many regions, especially during crises, patients or their families struggle to find available hospital beds or timely ambulance services. RakshakLink addresses this problem by creating a unified, intelligent platform that enables real-time coordination and faster decision-making.

The application allows users to trigger an emergency request through a simple SOS button, which automatically captures their location and sends it to the backend system. Using smart dispatch logic, the platform identifies the nearest available ambulance and matches it with the most suitable hospital based on real-time bed availability, including ICU, oxygen, and ventilator support. This ensures that patients are not only transported quickly but also taken to a facility that can actually treat them.

One of the key innovations of RakshakLink is its AI-assisted triage system. During transit, paramedics can input patient data or capture images, enabling the system to assess the severity of the condition and notify the hospital in advance. This helps emergency departments prepare before the patient arrives, significantly reducing response time and improving outcomes.

The system integrates modern technologies such as Android development (Kotlin), Firebase for real-time database and notifications, and Google Maps for location tracking and navigation. Designed as a scalable solution, RakshakLink can be expanded from city-level deployments to nationwide healthcare networks.

By combining real-time data, AI intelligence, and seamless communication, RakshakLink aims to save lives by making emergency healthcare faster, smarter, and more efficient.
